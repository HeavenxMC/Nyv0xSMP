// about

Nyv0x runs on one map, one economy, and one rule set for as long as people keep showing up. It's built for players who want their base to still be there in six months — no seasonal wipes, no pay-to-win, no drama nobody signed up for. Just replace this paragraph with your own server's story.

// features

What you're logging into

SURVIVAL

Vanilla-plus core

Light quality-of-life datapacks only — no gameplay-altering plugins. If it works in single-player, it works here.

ECONOMY

Player-run market

Shops are built and priced by players, not admins. Trade routes and prices are entirely community driven.

EVENTS

Community events

Regular build contests, boss fights, and market days — schedule posted in Discord every week.

STAFF

Active, low-ego mods

Small staff team that plays on the same map as everyone else and actually answers reports.

UPTIME

Backed up, always on

Hourly backups and a host that doesn't go down mid-build. Your work is never one crash from gone.

// rules

Before you spawn in

$

Be someone people want to build near. Harassment, hate speech, and chat drama get you removed — no warnings needed for the serious stuff.

$

No griefing, stealing, or hacked clients. Claim your land early. Unclaimed builds are fair game for reclaiming resources, not destruction.

$

Lag machines get removed on sight. Farms are welcome; farms that halve everyone's tick rate are not.

$

Keep builds within a reasonable distance of spawn. Nobody wants to render a base at coordinate 40,000.

$

Disputes go to staff, not chat. Screenshot it, DM a mod, let them sort it out.

Spawn is waiting.

Grab the whitelist form in Discord, drop your username, and you'll usually be added within the day.
